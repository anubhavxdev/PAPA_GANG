Compatible Device Types

AquaSave works with the following water devices to track and optimize your water usage

Smart Shower Systems

Monitors water flow, temperature, and usage time.

Compatible brands: Hydro, AquaFlow, EcoShower

Faucet Monitors

Tracks water flow rate and total volume used.

Compatible brands: WaterSense, FlowTrack, H2Optimize

Appliance Monitors

Tracks water usage of washing machines, dishwashers.

Compatible brands: EcoWash, WiseWater, SmartFlow

Irrigation Controllers

Smart controllers for outdoor watering systems.

Compatible brands: RainSave, GardenFlow, AquaGarden

Toilet Monitors

Detects leaks and monitors flush volume.

Compatible brands: FlushSense, WaterGuard, LeakStop

Whole Home Systems

Comprehensive water monitoring for entire property.

Compatible brands: HomeFlow, WaterWatch, AquaHome
